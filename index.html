The Heritage

From a 7,000 square foot building to the largest bar in Green Bay.

Stadium View opened in September 1992. The original building was a women's fine clothing store that the Watson family converted into a sports bar in just twenty-eight days, one block from Lambeau Field. It started at 7,000 square feet.

What followed was three decades of building. Three event halls were added. Two more full-service kitchens. An expansion of the original sports bar floor. Indoor and outdoor beer gardens. A rooftop bar. Stadium View grew into the largest bar in Green Bay, a venue that can hold thousands on a single Packers home Sunday.

"There are very few places as big as Stadium View that have been able to stay in a family as long as we have."
From the Stadium View family history
The Watson Family

A three-generation, family-owned Green Bay business.

Stadium View has been a Watson family business since the day it opened in 1992. Jerry and Diane Watson founded it as a husband-and-wife team. Today their son, Bob Watson, and his wife, Amanda Watson, run the business alongside a management staff.

That continuity is rare for a venue this size. Most bars the scale of Stadium View are owned by groups, chains, or investors. Stadium View is still run by the family whose name has been on it since the conversion crew finished in twenty-eight days. The full three-generation family story, with names and dates, is the kind of detail that belongs in the heritage of the site, and it is something we confirm with the family at kickoff.

Frequently Asked

Everything fans and event planners ask before they book.

Is Stadium View really the largest bar in Green Bay?
Yes. Stadium View Bar & Restaurant is the largest bar in Green Bay, WI. It opened in September 1992 at 7,000 square feet and has expanded across three decades into five full-service bars, two interior floors, indoor and outdoor beer gardens, a rooftop bar, and 12,000 square feet of event halls. On a Packers home game it is not uncommon for six to ten thousand people to come through the gates.
Who owns Stadium View?
Stadium View is a three-generation, family-owned business. It was founded in September 1992 by Jerry and Diane Watson as a husband-and-wife team. Today it is run by their son Bob Watson and his wife Amanda Watson, along with a management staff. Stadium View has stayed in the Watson family since the day it opened, which is rare for a venue of its size.
How close is Stadium View to Lambeau Field?
Stadium View is one block from Lambeau Field, at 1963 Holmgren Way, Green Bay, WI 54304. It is also just steps from the Resch Center and sits in Green Bay's Titletown stadium district. Lambeau Field opened in 1957 as City Stadium and was renamed Lambeau Field in 1965. Stadium View has been one block away since 1992, which makes it one of the most natural pre-game and post-game stops for Packers fans.
Can I host a wedding or private event at Stadium View?
Yes. Stadium View Event Halls is a separate side of the business with 12,000 square feet of newly renovated banquet and event space. It hosts weddings, receptions, rehearsal dinners, anniversary parties, corporate events, fundraisers, and large private celebrations. The event halls run independently of the sports bar floor, with their own catering and event menus. The production site surfaces current capacities, seasonal availability, and booking lead times.
What is Stadium View like on a Packers gameday?
On a Packers home Sunday, Stadium View runs all five full-service bars, both interior floors, the beer gardens, and the rooftop, with a dedicated game-day menu and gameday drink specials. It is one block from Lambeau Field, so it works as both a pre-game and post-game stop. Six to eight thousand, sometimes as many as ten thousand, fans come through the gates on a single home game. Groups should reserve in advance.
Does Stadium View show away games and host watch parties?
Yes. Stadium View is a watch-party venue for every Packers game, home and away, with two floors of screens, five bars, and beer gardens. When the Packers play away, Stadium View becomes one of the largest places in Green Bay to watch the game with a crowd. Group watch-party reservations can be made in the bar or the event halls.
