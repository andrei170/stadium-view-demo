AI search still names other venues first.

We asked ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews five buyer queries: Packers gameday bar Green Bay, bar near Lambeau Field, Green Bay event hall, banquet hall Green Bay, watch party venue Green Bay. Stadium View surfaced in one of five, and only on a general Green Bay bar list, never as the answer to the gameday or event query.

The other four answers went to 1919 Kitchen & Tap, D2 Sports Pub, Anduzzi's, and Hagemeister Park. None of them is the largest bar in Green Bay. None has a three-generation family story or an 8,000-strong Instagram audience. They are winning because their structured data tells AI engines they exist for those queries. The fix is not a rebuild. It is schema, a gameday-destination page, an event-hall funnel, and bridging the social audience into search.

The 90-day plan.

Four phases. The Packers-gameday-destination page ships in less than 7 days. AEO infrastructure and EventVenue schema deploy in week two. The event-hall funnel and the Instagram-to-search bridge follow. By day 90, Stadium View is cited in 4 of 5 buyer queries.

Phase 01

Packers Gameday Destination Page

A dedicated gameday page targeting "bar near Lambeau Field" and "Packers gameday bar Green Bay." Five-bar layout, gameday menu, reservations, one-block-from-Lambeau positioning. LocalBusiness and BarOrPub schema layered.

Week 1
Phase 02

AEO Infrastructure + EventVenue Schema

FAQPage schema, LocalBusiness schema separating the sports bar from the event halls, and EventVenue schema on the 12,000 sq. ft. banquet space. 40-word answer blocks on top pages. Search Console and Bing wired.

Week 2 to 3
Phase 03

Event-Hall + Watch-Party Funnels

An event-hall and banquet booking funnel with capacities and an inquiry form, plus dedicated watch-party-venue content for home and away games. The two highest-margin surfaces Stadium View is currently invisible for.

Weeks 3 to 6
Phase 04

Watson Story + Social-to-Search Bridge

The three-generation Watson family story built into Person schema and a heritage page. The 8,000-strong Instagram audience bridged into citation density: local press, Travel Wisconsin, Discover Green Bay, Packers fan media.
